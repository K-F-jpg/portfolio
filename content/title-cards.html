Title Cards & Animation
Core I
paper
3 printed posters, After Effects animation
For this assignment, we had to create 3 title cards from 3 screenshots of a music video. I chose Magdalena Bay's "Secrets: Your Fire", and used this assignment as a means of testing my flexibility in design thinking. We were also tasked with creating a short animation using one of our 3 title cards in Adobe AfterEffects; I chose the first in the series (featured last in the image sequence to the left), which you can watch by clicking on the link here.
