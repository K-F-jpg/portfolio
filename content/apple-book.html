"Take a Slice"
PHTO 491 // Mutant Book
tabloid magazine
1 artist book
This is a book inspired by the sin Adam and Eve committed in the garden of Eden by eating the apple from the tree of life after being tempted by the serpent. This concept is adopted into the modern day in the form of the tabloid magazine I used to create this project, representing frivolous pursuits that take hold of you by tempting you and then making it hard to leave/ escape when their full effect takes hold. The pages are in a multiple of 6 to represent the number of man. Feel free to watch the flipthrough of this book here.
