This is a book inspired by the original sin of Adam and Eve in the garden of Eden where they ate the apple from the tree of life after being tempted by the serpent. This concept is adopted into the modern day in the form of the tabloid magazine I used to create this project, representing the frivolous pursuits of the modern world manifesting in fashion, celebrity drama, and mindless comparison to a life of luxury. The pages are in a multiple of 6 to represent the number of man. Feel free to watch the flipthrough of this book here or peruse the spreads to the left.
