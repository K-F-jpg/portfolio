This is a book inspired by the original sin of Adam and Eve in the garden of Eden where they ate the apple from the tree of life after being tempted by the serpent. This concept is adopted into the modern day taking the form of the tabloid magazine I used to create this project, representing the frivolous pursuits of the modern world manifesting in fashion, celebrity drama, and mindless indulgence in luxurious lifestyles. The pages are in a multiple of 6 to represent the number of man and further connect the project to its Biblical roots.
