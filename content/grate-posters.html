Grate Posters
SUA25
120lb Stonehenge paper
2 screenprints
6)
These were also created during my May-June studies in Italy with the VCU Graphic Design program. I created two custom screenprints of a collection of historical wall-integrated vent systems I found throughout my travels that I outlined in Illustrator and assembled into a pattern. I printed these on textured paper to emulate the walls in the showcase room, printing one with a faulty screen to mimic the ancient broken walls found across Italy to play into their historical background.
