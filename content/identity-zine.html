Identity Zine
Multi Studio // Leave-Behind
tabloid magazine, apple template
We were required to create a zine, or some kind of publication, that talked about our identities in some way to act as a "leave-behind" for people who wanted to get to know us better; kinda like a business card, but more artsy. I took a few weeks to make a zine that I believed reflected my personality/ identity, making it resemble a laptop/ desktop to capture my digital identity. On the back, I added a magenta background and in front of that collaged some text from the front of the zine.
  • text created from screenshots of meme text and assembled/ collaged
  • the original project had sticky notes with qr codes pasted on them that lead to a TECHNOCORE music playlist.
  • the text on the back uses the same method as the front text to assemble the landing page to my portfolio, that you could plug into VScode or another text editor to get my landing page. It was completed with a google doc on instructions to use text editors to code the page (which you can actually click from here!).
On the page featuring the collaged text, I also include a link to a google doc that walks the viewer through setting up an IDE and typing in the code to get a functioning website.
